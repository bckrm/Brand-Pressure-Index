§ 04 · Method & Cadence

Four weeks. One clear answer.

The BPI is powered by Backroom's scoring engine. Every score is evidence-based, weighted to the pressure moment, and sorted for priority — so the worst score on the most critical dimension surfaces first.

Priority Formula how the engine sorts
Priority = ( 4 − score ) × weight
score 1–4, evidence-based. A 1 is broken; a 4 is holding.
weight 1.0× default. 1.5× if this dimension is heavy for your pressure moment.
sort Highest priority surfaces first. The worst score on the most critical dimension gets the top of the page.

The full engagement is four weeks, end to end. Each stage narrows the question until a single recommendation remains.

Stress Test

A 16-question diagnostic establishes the pressure moment and flags which dimensions are most exposed. Directional only.

§ 05 · Fit

Built for decisions, not presentations.

The BPI is a diagnostic instrument, not a brand deck. It produces a scored read on where the brand will hold or fail — and a single recommendation on what to fix first. If that's not what you're looking for, this isn't the right tool.

— Built for

Founders, CEOs, and operating partners facing a pressure moment.

  • Preparing for a sale, raise, or exit
  • Stepping into a leadership transition
  • Inside a post-acquisition integration
  • Feeling the brand outpaced by the company
  • Deciding whether a rebrand is actually the problem
— Not built for

Teams shopping for a logo refresh or a brand deck.

  • CMOs looking for a design sprint
  • Marketing teams wanting a campaign idea
  • Companies without a pressure moment to navigate
  • Anyone looking for a rebrand pitch disguised as strategy
  • Organizations that want their self-perception confirmed
